Lasagna!

A close perspective of lasagna. This lasagna was made with all natural beef.

Ingredients for recipes

  • Ground Beef
  • Tomato
  • Carrots
  • Green Peppers
  • Lasagna Pasta
  • Cheese

Steps

  1. Salt and boil water. Add lasagna to rapidly boiling water; Cook according to manufacturer's directions
  2. Saute ground beef, carrots, and green peppers, season with salt/pepper. Cook until meat is carmelized.
  3. Layer cooked pasta on bottom of baking dish, spread a layer of cheese, and add a layer of meat mixture; repeat until no more remains.
  4. Finish layering with a large amount of cheese on top
  5. Pre-heat oven to 450 degrees
  6. Bake Lasanga for 18 minutes, or until edges start to carmelize and turn golden-brown. Allow to cool for 15 minutes, cut and serve.
